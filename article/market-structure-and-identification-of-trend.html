Market Structure and Identification of trend

Created At： 2024-09-01 03:47 Updated At： 2024-09-01 07:18

Fundamentally, the market is consistently engaged in buying (up) and selling (down) activities, with no periods of complete stillness except during market closures or spread hours.Typically, the market is engaged in one of two activities:

  • The market can be Trending – Uptrend and Downtrend.
  • The market can be Consolidating – Ranging, Choppy, Unclear.

Trending Market Uptrend

An uptrend is characterized as a market structure that breaks the highs while maintaining the lows. Put simply, it signifies a market that achieves higher highs and higher lows. The previous high (HH) should always be surpassed, while the previous low (HL) should not be breached in an uptrend.

Trending Market Downtrend

A downtrend is characterized by a market pattern that breaks previous lows and sustains lower highs. A downtrend is described as a market structure that breaks the lows while maintaining the highs. The previous low (LL) should always be breached, while the previous high (LH) should remain intact and not be broken in a downtrend.

Consolidating Market

Consolidation is described as a market structure that fails to break either the highs or lows. Before every significant movement, there is typically a phase of consolidation, followed by a substantial liquidity grab, and then the actual significant move occurs.
